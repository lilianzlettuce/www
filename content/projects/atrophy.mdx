      Atrophy explores corruption in relation to the human condition with a sort of twisted Hero’s Journey. In the place of a promised destiny, this unwinnable game tells the story of a forsaken belief that does not match reality.
      The player is immersed in a world featuring vastly contrasting environments, one a post-apocalyptic terrain harboring an ever-growing amalgamation of conflict, the other an artificially picturesque landscape reflective of the idyllic self. They must choose between fighting a battle they cannot win, or submerging themselves within a delusional dream.
