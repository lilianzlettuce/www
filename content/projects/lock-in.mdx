- It works pretty well but the one thing that ruins the whole thing for me is that you can only have ONE layer of nesting
- This sucks!!! My lists are an unorganized mess
- But also I don't want to switch to an entire new app
- Which is why... I decided to make my own Todo List feature with an infinite amount of nesting :D
- Sounds pretty easy right? It actually ended up being much harder than expected. And probably one of the only times I've actually needed to use recursion ever
