      This augmented reality experience examines the subtle sinister side of technology as an object of worship. It blends reality with our minds’ perception through the use of scannable, code-drawn stickers that can be placed on everyday objects, personal devices, and city infrastructure. The digital artifacts that emerge offer a visual representation of a threat that is otherwise invisible, manifesting into reality both our unavoidable attachment to our devices and the ever-existing potential danger they pose to our privacy.
